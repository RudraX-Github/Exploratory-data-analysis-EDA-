Case Study 01

NYC Smart Predictor

Predicting the Pulse of New York City. Transforming millions of raw GPS logs into 97% accurate fare predictions using XGBoost and 3D Spatial Mapping.

Mission Directive

The objective was to architect a machine learning pipeline capable of decoding the complex pulse of NYC transit. By analyzing factors such as distance, traffic logic, and borough-specific rules, we engineered a system that doesn't just guess fares—it understands the context of every trip.

97%R² Score
100k+Data Points
40+Landmarks
3DSpatial Map

System Evolution

Phase 1: Discovery

Data Auditing & Decoding

Ingested `Green_Data.csv` and implemented automated audit protocols. Decoded cryptic RateCodeIDs (e.g., JFK Airport zones) and visualized distribution skews to establish a clean baseline.

Phase 2: The Science

Feature Engineering & XGBoost

Extracted complex temporal metrics. Comparative testing between Linear Regression and Decision Trees revealed XGBoost as the champion for non-linear pattern recognition.

Phase 3: Architecture

Logic Layer Implementation

Constructed the bridge between user inputs and the model matrix. Implemented Haversine formulas for geospatial distance calculation and traffic logic simulators.

Phase 4: UX V7.0

3D Visualization & Insights

Final production release featuring PyDeck 3D Arc Layers for glowing path visualization over a pitch-black map. Replaced raw coordinates with smart landmark selectors.
